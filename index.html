Crystal Collector





You will be given a randon number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals above. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount to your final score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes about the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
